Additional Details: This clinic is offering the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine which is for people 16 years and older only. This clinic is for people who are 60 years and older who live, work, or go to school in Rhode Island and people who are 16 years and older with one or more qualifying underlying medical conditions* who live, work, or go to school in Rhode Island. It is also for people who were previously invited by the Rhode Island Department of Health to be vaccinated as a member of a group prioritized for vaccination in Phase 1 of Rhode Island’s vaccination campaign. For clinic directions and parking information, visit https://health.ri.gov/vaccinesiteinfo. *Visit https://health.ri.gov/underlyingconditions to see a list of qualifying medical conditions. Please do not arrive more than 15 minutes before your appointment. If you arrive earlier, you may not be able to park and may be asked to come back later. There is enough vaccine for everyone who has an appointment. For GPS directions enter: 1400 West Main Rd, Middletown, RI. The clinic resides in the former Benny’s department store. Please enter the shopping plaza through the main entrance and head towards the right, where there will be available parking for clinic patients. There will be proper signage designating where the entrance and exit will be. You will get your COVID-19 vaccine in your upper arm. Please wear a shirt with loose-fitting sleeves or with a sleeve that can be easily rolled up. We try very hard to minimize waiting time; however, sometimes delays happen. If you are not able to stand for a period of time, please bring a wheelchair or a walker with a seat, if you have one.

If you need to cancel, please click on “Cancel” link in your confirmation email. If you need to change your appointment time, please click on “Reschedule” in your confirmation email link to check if there are other time slots available. If you need to change the date, cancel this appointment and schedule another one. We cannot guarantee that you will be able to reschedule for the desired date and time. If you need assistance please call 844-930-1779.
